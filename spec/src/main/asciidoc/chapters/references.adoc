:fnConnectionSemantics: footnote:[In this document, the term 'physical connection' refers to a `ManagedConnection` instance, while the term 'connection handle' refers to an application-level connection handle. When the distinction between 'physical connection' and 'connection handle' is not important, the term 'connection' is used to refer to an EIS connection.]
